ABOUT

01 / THE OVERVIEW

Building systems through code, design, and constant iteration.

I develop digital experiences that aim to be structured, intuitive, and responsive—where logic and interface move together. The focus is always the same: take complex ideas and turn them into something usable, without losing depth or intent.

Still figuring things out, refining the process, and pushing each build a little further than the last—treating every project as both practice and proof of growth.

02 / THE PROCESS

Away from the screen, I step back, observe, and let ideas settle—paying attention to how things work, how people move through them, and where they break or feel right. That space outside of coding is where perspective builds, and where direction becomes clearer before anything is written.

I’m grateful for the chance to learn through every build—the small wins, the mistakes, and the people and resources that keep pushing me forward.
